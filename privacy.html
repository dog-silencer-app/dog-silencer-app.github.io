Dog Silencer captures audio for the purposes of determining volume to establish when a dog is barking so as to know when to emit a sound to encourage the dog to quieten down. Audio samples are not kept once the volume has been measured. No samples are stored in memory or to disk. No samples are sent over any network or other data connection, either wired or wireless. If auto-pause on notification is selected then the read notifications permission will be requested. This is used only to determine when notifications arrive for the purpose of pausing the silencer. No access to the contents of notifications is made. If auto-pause on call is selected then the phone state permission will be requested. This is used only to determine when calls are in progress for the purpose of pausing the silencer. The requested permission does not grant access to phone numbers or call history.
